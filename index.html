Case Study: Resolved Analytics

The Problem

Resolved Analytics faced an initial hurdle with their newly built website: blog posts were under-optimized and there was an absence of a robust technical SEO framework. This was impeding website traffic and hindering conversion rates.

The Solution

Phase One addressed the technical SEO by implementing canonical tags to resolve duplications and enhancing site navigation with internal linking. Phase Two involved auditing content and refining the strategy, optimizing existing blogs, and producing targeted new articles to bolster traffic and conversions.

Results

  • Doubled conversion rates
  • Significant increase in qualified traffic
  • Improved technical SEO foundation
  • Enhanced content performance metrics
